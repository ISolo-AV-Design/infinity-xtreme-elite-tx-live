Our Competitive Program

Meet the Teams

Four All-Star squads, one family. Each team carries its own colors under the Infinity Xtreme crest.

Stardust

Mini Exhibition

Our youngest competitors take the floor. Stardust introduces mini athletes to performance, teamwork, and the thrill of competition in a supportive exhibition setting.

Legacy

Youth Novice

Youth athletes building the foundation of All-Star cheer — clean technique, confident performance, and the teamwork that carries through every level.

Reckless

Junior Level 1 Elite

Junior athletes bringing energy and edge. Reckless competes at Level 1 Elite, sharpening stunts, tumbling, and choreography for the competitive floor.

Savage

Junior Level 2 Elite

Powerhouse Elite Level 2 all-star team. Combining razor-sharp synchronization, explosive tumbling, and elite stunting with high energy. Proving that you don't need the highest level to leave the biggest impact.
