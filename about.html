About Infinity Xtreme Elite TX

Family-founded, family-run, and built for every athlete who steps on our mat.

Mission

More than a cheer gym

At Infinity Xtreme Elite TX, we are more than a cheer gym — we are a family. Founded by families who share a passion for cheer, we are dedicated to developing strong athletes, confident leaders, and meaningful relationships that extend beyond the mat.

Through excellence in coaching, teamwork, and a supportive environment, we strive to help every athlete reach their full potential while creating a place where families feel connected, valued, and inspired.

Building champions on the mat, leaders in life, and family through cheer. ∞
